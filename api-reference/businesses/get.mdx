---
title: Get Business
api: GET https://dev.teelapp.io/api/businesses/{id}
description: Get a business by its unique identifier
---

Returns the details of a specific business by its ID.

## Authentication

This endpoint requires a valid JWT bearer token in the `Authorization` header.

## Path Parameters

<ParamField path="id" type="string" required>
  The unique identifier of the business.
</ParamField>

## Response

<ResponseField name="id" type="string">
  Unique business identifier.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="name" type="string">
  Legal business name.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="type" type="string">
  Business entity type.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="country" type="string">
  Country code.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="email" type="string">
  Contact email.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="phone" type="string">
  Contact phone number.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="createdAt" type="string">
  ISO 8601 timestamp of when the business was created.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="updatedAt" type="string">
  ISO 8601 timestamp of the last update.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseExample>

```json 200
{
  "id": "biz_xyz789",
  "name": "Acme Corp",

```
</ResponseExample>
