---
title: Register Business (KYB)
api: POST https://dev.teelapp.io/api/kyb/register
description: Register a business with all providers at once via KYB submission
---

Submits KYB (Know Your Business) information to register a business with all connected providers simultaneously. Requires the `X-User-ID` header to associate the registration with a user.

## Headers

<ParamField header="X-User-ID" type="string" required>
  The unique identifier of the user initiating the KYB registration.
</ParamField>

## Body Parameters

<ParamField body="businessName" type="string" required>
  Legal name of the business.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="businessType" type="string" required>
  Type of business entity (e.g. `llc`, `corporation`, `sole_proprietorship`).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="registrationNumber" type="string" required>
  Business registration or EIN number.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="country" type="string" required>
  ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 country code where the business is registered (e.g. `US`,
  `GB`).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="address" type="object" required>
  Registered business address.
  <Expandable title="Address fields">
    <ParamField body="street" type="string" required>
      Street address.
    </ParamField>
    <ParamField body="city" type="string" required>
      City name.
    </ParamField>
    <ParamField body="state" type="string">
      State or province.
    </ParamField>
    <ParamField body="postalCode" type="string" required>
      Postal or ZIP code.
    </ParamField>
    <ParamField body="country" type="string" required>
      Country code.
    </ParamField>
  </Expandable>
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="contactEmail" type="string" required>
  Primary contact email for the business.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="contactPhone" type="string">
  Primary contact phone number.
</ParamField>

## Response

<ResponseField name="registrationId" type="string">
  Unique identifier for this KYB registration.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="status" type="string">
  Overall registration status (e.g. `pending`, `approved`, `rejected`).
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="providers" type="object">
  Map of provider names to their individual registration statuses.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseExample>
```json 200
{
  "registrationId": "kyb_abc123def456",
  "status": "pending",
  "providers": {
    "conduit": "pending",
    "lemonado": "pending",
    "circle": "pending"
  }
}
```
</ResponseExample>
