Teel aggregates multiple payment providers behind a single API, giving you broad coverage across currencies, countries, and payment rails. The specific corridors available to your business depend on your KYB onboarding status.

## Payment Corridors

Teel supports four types of payment flows:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Fiat-to-Fiat">
    Cross-border bank transfers from one fiat currency to another. Teel handles
    the onramp and offramp legs automatically, selecting the best route.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fiat-to-Stablecoin">
    Onramp from fiat currency to stablecoins (USDC, USDT). Funds are deposited
    to a blockchain wallet address.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stablecoin-to-Fiat">
    Offramp from stablecoins to fiat currency. Stablecoins are converted and
    delivered to a bank account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stablecoin-to-Stablecoin">
    Cross-chain stablecoin transfers via bridge. Move tokens between supported
    blockchain networks.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Supported Currencies

Teel supports a wide range of currencies across multiple regions. Available corridors depend on your onboarding status.

| Region         | Currencies         | Payment Rails                          |
| -------------- | ------------------ | -------------------------------------- |
| North America  | USD                | ACH, Wire, SWIFT                       |
| Europe         | EUR, GBP           | SEPA, Faster Payments, SWIFT           |
| Southeast Asia | PHP, IDR, VND, MYR | Local bank transfer, InstaPay, PESONet |
| South Asia     | INR                | UPI, Bank Transfer                     |
| Latin America  | MXN, BRL           | Local bank transfer                    |
| Africa         | ZAR, NGN, KES, GHS | Local rails                            |

## Blockchain Networks

For stablecoin operations (onramp, offramp, and cross-chain transfers), Teel supports the following networks and tokens.

**Supported chains:**

- Ethereum
- Arbitrum
- Polygon
- Base

**Supported tokens:**

- USDC
- USDT
- MYRC

**Cross-chain transfers:**

- Native USDC transfers via CCTP (Circle)
- Fast bridging across supported chains

## Checking Available Currencies

To see which currencies are available based on your onboarding status, call the capabilities endpoint:

```bash
curl -X GET https://dev.teelapp.io/api/kyb/capabilities/currencies \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_TOKEN"
```

This returns the full list of currencies you can send and receive based on your current onboarding status.
